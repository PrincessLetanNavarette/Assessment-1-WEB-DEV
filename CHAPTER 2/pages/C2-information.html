A Walk To Remember
Information Overview Cast Reviews AWTR
    Author:
  • Nicholas Sparks
    Year:
  • 1999
    Characters:
  • Landon Carter and Jamie Sullivan
    Setting:
  • A small town in North Carolina
    Summary:
  • Landon, a popular high school senior, is forced to participate in the school play with Jamie, the minister's daughter. Despite their differences, they develop a deep connection and fall in love. However, their relationship faces challenges due to Jamie's deteriorating health. Love, faith, redemption, forgiveness
    Adaptation:
  • The novel was adapted into a film of the same name in 2002, starring Shane West as Landon and Mandy Moore as Jamie. The novel received positive reviews for its emotional depth and storytelling. The film adaptation garnered commercial success and has a dedicated fanbase.
