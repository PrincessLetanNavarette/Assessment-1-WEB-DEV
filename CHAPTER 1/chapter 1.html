How to Make a French Toast?

What you'll need:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon milk
  • 2-4 slices of bread
  • butter
  • cinnamon
  • maple syrup

Here are the steps:

  1. Break eggs into a wide, shallow bowl and beat lightly with a fork
  2. Stir in sugar, salt, and milk
  3. Place a slice of bread to bowl, letting each slice soak up egg mixture for a few seconds on each side.
  4. Over medium-low heat, heat griddle or skillet and coat with a thin layer of butte or margarine
  5. Carefully transfer bread slices to griddle or skillet
  6. Heat slowly until bottom is golden brown. Turn and brown the other side
  7. Sprinkle cinnamon lightly on both sides of each slice
  8. Serve French toast with butter and syrup.

Enjoy!
